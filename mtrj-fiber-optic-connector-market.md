<p><ul><li>Singlemode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MTRJ fiber optic connector market is divided into two main types: singlemode and multimode. Singlemode connectors are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a small core diameter to allow a single light mode to propagate, resulting in minimal signal loss. In contrast, multimode connectors feature a larger core diameter, enabling multiple light modes to transmit simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances. Both types serve different applications, with singlemode favored for telecommunications and multimode often used in local area networks.</p></p>
