<p><p>The plastic film market encompasses various types, including polyethylene (PE), bi-axially oriented polypropylene (BOPP), and bi-axially oriented polyethylene terephthalate (BOPET). PE films are known for their flexibility and moisture resistance, making them ideal for packaging and agricultural applications. BOPP films offer high clarity, strength, and heat resistance, commonly used in food packaging and labels. These films cater to diverse industries, driving market growth.</p></p>
