<p><p>Oocyte aspiration needles are essential in assisted reproductive technologies for retrieving oocytes from ovarian follicles. The market features two main types: single-lumen and double-lumen needles. Single-lumen needles have one channel for fluid aspiration, maximizing simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, double-lumen needles possess two channels, allowing simultaneous aspiration while infusing fluids, which can enhance follicle distension. Both types cater to different clinical needs and preferences, contributing to advancements in fertility treatments and improving patient outcomes in oocyte retrieval procedures.</p></p>
