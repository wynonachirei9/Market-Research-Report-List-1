<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Applied Membrane System market is categorized into three primary types: Bituminous, Elastomeric, and Cementitious. Bituminous membranes are derived from asphalt, providing strong waterproofing and durability for roofing applications. Elastomeric membranes are flexible and can stretch, making them ideal for varied temperature conditions and dynamic building movements. Cementitious membranes are based on cement, offering excellent adhesion and resistance to moisture, often used for water tanks and wet areas. Each type serves specific needs within construction and waterproofing projects.</p></p>
