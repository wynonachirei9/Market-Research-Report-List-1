<p><p>The MRI systems market primarily features two types: open and closed MRI systems. Open MRI systems have a wide, open design that reduces patient anxiety and is more accommodating for individuals with claustrophobia. However, they generally offer lower resolution images. In contrast, closed MRI systems are cylindrical and provide high-quality, detailed images but can be restrictive, leading to discomfort for some patients. Each type serves different clinical needs and patient preferences, influencing their market demand.</p></p>
