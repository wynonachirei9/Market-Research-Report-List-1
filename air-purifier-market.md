<p><p>The air purifier market features various technologies, each designed for specific air quality needs. HEPA purifiers excel at capturing airborne particles, including dust and allergens. Activated carbon filters absorb odors and volatile organic compounds. UV technology targets pathogens by using ultraviolet light to sterilize the air. Ionizers release charged particles to attract and remove pollutants, while ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors. Other market types may include electrostatic or combination purifiers, offering versatile solutions for improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
