<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Dual Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Implantable Cardiac Defibrillator (ICD) market comprises primarily two types: Single Chamber and Dual Chamber ICDs. Single Chamber ICDs are designed to monitor and treat arrhythmias in one heart chamber, typically the right ventricle. In contrast, Dual Chamber ICDs monitor both the atrium and ventricle, allowing for more precise rhythm management and synchronization of the heart's chambers. These types cater to different patient needs, with Dual Chamber ICDs generally providing enhanced therapy options for complex arrhythmias.</p></p>
