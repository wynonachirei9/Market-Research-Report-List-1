<p><p>The mosquito repellent market is segmented into several types: coils, vaporizers, mats, aerosols, and creams. Coils release smoke that repels mosquitoes when burned, while vaporizers emit insecticide-filled vapors for effective coverage. Mats utilize heat to release repellents gradually, providing long-lasting protection. Aerosols offer convenient spraying options for immediate relief, and creams are applied directly to the skin for personal protection. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios, addressing varying effectiveness and convenience needs.</p></p>
