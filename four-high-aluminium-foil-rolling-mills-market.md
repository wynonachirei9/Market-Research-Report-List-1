<p><p>The Four-High Aluminium Foil Rolling Mills market comprises three distinct types: Roughing Mills, Intermediate Mills, and Finishing Mills. Roughing Mills are used for initial thick metal processing, preparing the material for further reduction. Intermediate Mills focus on reducing thickness while maintaining gauge control, smoothing the material for final processing. Finishing Mills perform the final rolling stages, achieving the desired thickness and surface quality of aluminium foil. Each type plays a critical role in aluminium production, ensuring high-quality end products.</p></p>
