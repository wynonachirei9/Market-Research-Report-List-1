<p><p>The solar on-grid inverter market comprises three main types: micro, string, and central inverters. Microinverters are small devices attached to each solar panel, optimizing energy conversion individually, making them ideal for residential setups with shading issues. String inverters connect multiple panels in series, offering a cost-effective solution for medium to large installations, balancing performance and simplicity. Central inverters are large systems designed for utility-scale projects, providing high efficiency and managing significant amounts of energy from numerous solar panels simultaneously.</p></p>
