<p><p>The color dispersions market is categorized primarily into water-based and UV-curable types. Water-based dispersions are formulated using water as the solvent, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for various applications in coatings, paints, and inks. On the other hand, UV-curable dispersions utilize ultraviolet light to initiate a curing process, resulting in quick-drying coatings that offer excellent durability and resistance to chemicals. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods, highlighting their unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
