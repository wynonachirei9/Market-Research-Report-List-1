<p><p>The ball valve seat market is divided into two primary types: soft valve seats and metal valve seats. Soft valve seats, typically made from materials like rubber or plastic, provide excellent sealing capabilities and are ideal for low-pressure applications, offering ease of installation and maintenance. In contrast, metal valve seats, crafted from materials such as stainless steel or other alloys, are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, delivering enhanced durability and resistance to wear and corrosion, making them suitable for harsh environments.</p></p>
