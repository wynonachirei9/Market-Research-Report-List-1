<p><ul><li>Single-stage Cordless Snow Blower</li><li>Two-stage Cordless Snow Blower</li><li>Three-stage Cordless Snow Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cordless snow blower market is segmented into three main types: single-stage, two-stage, and three-stage models. Single-stage cordless snow blowers are lightweight and designed for light snow, using an auger to both gather and throw snow. Two-stage models offer increased power, utilizing an auger and a separate impeller for deeper snow and larger areas. Three-stage cordless snow blowers provide the most efficient snow removal, incorporating an additional auger for faster processing, suitable for heavy snowfall and tough conditions.</p></p>
