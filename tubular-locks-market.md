<p><ul><li>Tubular Slam Lock</li><li>Tubular Switch Lock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tubular locks market includes two main types: tubular slam locks and tubular switch locks. Tubular slam locks are designed for high-security applications where quick locking is essential, often used in doors requiring rapid access. They typically feature a spring-loaded mechanism that secures the lock when the door closes. In contrast, tubular switch locks are commonly used in electronic devices or appliances, providing a secure means of controlling machinery or access by requiring a specific tubular key to operate.</p></p>
