<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photosensitive Reversible</li><li>Photosensitive Irreversible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive polymer market is categorized into two main types: photosensitive reversible and photosensitive irreversible polymers. Photosensitive reversible polymers undergo changes upon exposure to light but can return to their original state when light is removed, making them suitable for applications requiring reusability. In contrast, photosensitive irreversible polymers undergo permanent changes upon light exposure, leading to fixed structures, ideal for applications in lithography and durable coatings. Both types are essential in diverse fields such as electronics, healthcare, and packaging.</p></p>
