<p><p>The commercial vehicle tire valve market comprises two main types: rubber tire valves and metal tire valves. Rubber tire valves are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to corrosion, making them popular for various applications in vehicles. They provide effective sealing and are often cost-effective. In contrast, metal tire valves are more durable and resistant to high temperatures and pressures, offering enhanced performance in demanding conditions. Their robustness makes them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles, contributing to their growing preference among commercial operators.</p></p>
