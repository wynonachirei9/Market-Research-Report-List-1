<p><ul><li>Non-Disconnected Transaxle</li><li>Disconnect Transaxle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transaxle market comprises two primary types: non-disconnected transaxles and disconnected transaxles. Non-disconnected transaxles integrate the transmission and differential in a single unit, offering compactness and efficiency, commonly used in smaller vehicles. Disconnected transaxles, on the other hand, feature a separable design, allowing for greater flexibility and the ability to disengage the powertrain during specific conditions, enhancing fuel efficiency and performance. Both types serve distinct applications, catering to diverse automotive needs and preferences.</p></p>
