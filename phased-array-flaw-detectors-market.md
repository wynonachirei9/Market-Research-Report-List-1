<p><p>Phased Array Flaw Detectors are advanced ultrasonic testing devices used for detecting flaws in materials. The market is segmented into two main types: Linear Array and Circular Array. Linear Array detectors utilize a straight-line configuration of transducers, offering high resolution for scanning larger areas, making them suitable for weld inspections and flat surfaces. In contrast, Circular Array detectors feature transducers arranged in a circular pattern, allowing for enhanced coverage and detection of flaws in complex geometries, such as pipes and tanks.</p></p>
