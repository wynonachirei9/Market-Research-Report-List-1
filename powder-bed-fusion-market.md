<p><p>The Powder Bed Fusion market encompasses several advanced 3D printing techniques including Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM), which both utilize lasers to fuse metal powders for high-density components. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) applies to polymers, enabling the creation of complex geometries. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) uses an electron beam for metal powder melting, offering enhanced precision. Selective Heat Sintering (SHS) employs heat to bond materials, focusing on non-metallic applications, thus broadening operational capabilities across industries.</p></p>
