<p><p>The industrial medium-voltage cables market encompasses various types, including XLPE cables, MI cables, and EPR cables. XLPE cables, or cross-linked polyethylene cables, offer excellent thermal resistance and electrical properties, making them suitable for outdoor and underground applications. MI cables, or mineral insulated cables, provide superior fire resistance and are ideal for harsh environments. EPR cables, made from ethylene propylene rubber, possess good flexibility and moisture resistance, making them suitable for diverse industrial applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
