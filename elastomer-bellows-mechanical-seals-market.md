<p><p>Elastomer bellows mechanical seals are divided into stationary and rotary types, each serving distinct applications in sealing solutions. Stationary seals remain fixed in place, preventing fluid leakage in equipment like pumps and compressors, providing robust reliability in demanding environments. Rotary seals, on the other hand, allow for motion between rotating and stationary components, effectively sealing rotating shafts while accommodating dynamic conditions. The choice between these types depends on specific operational requirements, including pressure, temperature, and motion characteristics.</p></p>
