<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>PLC Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Horizontal Metal Baler market is categorized into three main types: manual, PLC automatic, and hybrid systems. Manual balers require human operation for loading, compressing, and unloading materials, offering simplicity and lower costs. PLC automatic balers utilize programmable logic controls for efficient and precise operation, enhancing productivity and reducing labor costs. Hybrid systems combine manual and automated features, allowing for flexibility in operation while maintaining efficiency. Each type serves different customer needs based on operational scale and budget.</p></p>
