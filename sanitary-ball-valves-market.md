<p><p>The sanitary ball valves market consists primarily of two connection types: clamp connections and weld connections. Clamp connections facilitate easy installation and maintenance, allowing for quick disassembly without tools, making them popular in applications where frequent cleaning is required. Weld connections, on the other hand, provide a permanent solution with superior strength and minimal leakage risk, ideal for high-pressure applications in industries like food and beverage and pharmaceuticals. Each type caters to specific operational needs and regulatory standards.</p></p>
