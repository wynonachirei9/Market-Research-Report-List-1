<p><p>The ranging optical sensor market primarily comprises laser and LED technologies. Laser sensors utilize coherent light for precise distance measurement, offering high accuracy and long-range capabilities, making them ideal for applications like industrial automation and robotics. LED sensors, on the other hand, use incoherent light and are generally more cost-effective, suitable for shorter distances and applications such as proximity sensing. Both types cater to different needs in sectors like automotive, consumer electronics, and environmental monitoring, enhancing measurement efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
