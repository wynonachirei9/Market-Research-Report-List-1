<p><p>The screen protector market is primarily divided into three types: PET, tempered glass, and others. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) protectors are lightweight, flexible, and offer moderate scratch resistance. Tempered glass protectors provide superior durability and shatter resistance while maintaining touch sensitivity. The "others" category includes specialty protectors like anti-blue light or privacy screens, catering to specific needs. Each type serves different consumer preferences based on protection level, aesthetics, and functionality, contributing to a diverse market landscape.</p></p>
