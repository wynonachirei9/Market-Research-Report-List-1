<p><p>The eyebrow enhancing serum market consists of three main types: peptide-based, vitamin-based, and prostaglandin-derived formulas. Peptide-based serums utilize amino acids to stimulate hair growth and improve thickness, promoting healthier eyebrows. Vitamin-based serums harness essential nutrients, such as biotin and vitamins E and B, to nourish and strengthen existing hair. Prostaglandin-derived serums contain compounds that enhance hair growth by extending the growth phase of hair follicles, providing denser and more voluminous eyebrows. Each category caters to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
