<p><p>The technical glass market comprises several key types, each with distinct properties and applications. Borosilicate glass is known for its high thermal resistance, making it ideal for laboratory and kitchenware. Aluminosilicate glass offers enhanced durability and is often used in high-stress environments. Soda-lime glass is the most common type, utilized in containers and flat glass products. Lead-alkali-silicate glass provides excellent optical clarity, suitable for optical applications. Ceramic glass combines aesthetic appeal with thermal stability, often used in cookware and decorative items.</p></p>
