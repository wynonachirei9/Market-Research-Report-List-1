<p><p>The composting toilet market features two primary types: self-contained and split composting toilets. A self-contained composting toilet integrates all components in a single unit, making it suitable for smaller spaces and easier to install. Conversely, a split composting toilet separates the toilet from the composting chamber, typically placed underground or in a different location, facilitating larger waste processing and enhancing ventilation. Both types promote sustainable waste management by converting human waste into compost, reducing reliance on traditional plumbing systems.</p></p>
