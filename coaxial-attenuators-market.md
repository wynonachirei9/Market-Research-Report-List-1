<p><ul><li>Fixed Coaxial Attenuators</li><li>Step Coaxial Attenuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coaxial attenuator market is categorized into two primary types: fixed coaxial attenuators and step coaxial attenuators. Fixed coaxial attenuators provide a consistent level of attenuation across a range of frequencies and are used in applications where a stable signal reduction is required. In contrast, step coaxial attenuators offer variable levels of attenuation in discrete steps, allowing users to adjust the signal strength more flexibly. Both types are essential for managing signal levels in various RF and microwave applications.</p></p>
