<p><p>The human sensor market is primarily driven by two types: pyroelectric infrared sensors and thermopile sensors. Pyroelectric infrared sensors detect motion and presence by sensing changes in infrared radiation, making them ideal for security and automation applications. In contrast, thermopile sensors measure temperature variations by detecting infrared radiation emitted from objects, allowing for non-contact temperature monitoring. Both sensor types enhance energy efficiency and automation in smart buildings, healthcare, and consumer electronics, catering to growing demand for advanced sensing technologies.</p></p>
