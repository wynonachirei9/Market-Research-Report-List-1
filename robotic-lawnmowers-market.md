<p><ul><li>Pivoting Blades System</li><li>Fixed Blades System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic lawnmowers market can be categorized into two main types based on their cutting systems: pivoting blades and fixed blades. Pivoting blades systems utilize curved blades that rotate on a pivot for enhanced maneuverability, allowing them to navigate complex terrains and deliver a more precise cut. In contrast, fixed blade systems use stationary blades that maintain a consistent height, offering simplicity and reliability. Each system caters to different consumer preferences, with pivoting blades often preferred for intricate lawns and fixed blades for straightforward applications.</p></p>
