<p><p>The colonoscopy devices market is segmented into two main types: CT scanners and MRI scanners for colonoscopy. CT scanners utilize computed tomography to create detailed images of the colon, aiding in the detection of abnormalities such as polyps or tumors. Conversely, MRI scanners use magnetic resonance imaging, providing high-resolution images without radiation exposure, making it ideal for assessing soft tissue. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and contribute to effective colorectal cancer screening and management.</p></p>
