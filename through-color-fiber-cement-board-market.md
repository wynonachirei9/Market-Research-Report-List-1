<p><ul><li>Thin Board</li><li>Conventional Board</li><li>Thick Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Through-color fiber cement boards are categorized into three market types based on thickness. Thin boards, typically around 4-6mm thick, are lightweight and ideal for interior applications, providing versatility in design. Conventional boards, usually 8-12mm thick, offer a balance of durability and aesthetic appeal, making them suitable for both interior and exterior uses. Thick boards, exceeding 12mm, provide enhanced strength and are often used in high-impact areas or for structural applications, ensuring long-lasting performance in various environments.</p></p>
