<p><p>The Single Core Underground Cabling EPC market features three primary types of cable insulation materials: PVC, XLPE, and Rubber. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) cables are cost-effective, flexible, and suitable for various applications but have limited thermal resistance. XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) offers superior thermal and chemical resistance, making it ideal for high-voltage applications. Rubber cables provide excellent flexibility and durability in harsh environments, though they can be more expensive. Each type caters to specific installation needs and operational requirements in underground cabling projects.</p></p>
