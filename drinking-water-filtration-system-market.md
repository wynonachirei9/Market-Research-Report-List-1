<p><p>The drinking water filtration system market includes several types, primarily featuring reverse osmosis (RO) water filters, ultrafiltration membrane water filters, and others. RO systems use a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities and contaminants from water, providing high-quality filtration. Ultrafiltration membranes operate with slightly larger pore sizes, effectively filtering out bacteria and large particles while preserving beneficial minerals. Other filtration methods may include activated carbon filters and sediment filters, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences for clean drinking water.</p></p>
