<p><p>Wet blasting equipment operates primarily in two markets: slurry and vapor. The slurry market involves a mixture of water, abrasives, and air, creating a wet mixture for surface treatment, effective in reducing dust and enhancing visibility. In contrast, the vapor market employs a water mist that generates fine droplets, minimizing water usage while still delivering surface cleaning and finishing. Both methods aim to improve the efficiency of blasting processes while reducing environmental impact and enhancing worker safety.</p></p>
