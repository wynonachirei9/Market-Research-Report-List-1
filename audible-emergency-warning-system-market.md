<p><ul><li>Omni Directional</li><li>Rotating Directional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Audible Emergency Warning System market is characterized by two primary types: Omni Directional and Rotating Directional systems. Omni Directional systems emit sound uniformly in all directions, ensuring widespread alert coverage and making them ideal for open areas. In contrast, Rotating Directional systems focus sound in specific directions, rotating to project warnings more effectively over larger distances. This targeted approach is beneficial in urban settings where sound may need to reach specific locations while minimizing noise pollution elsewhere.</p></p>
