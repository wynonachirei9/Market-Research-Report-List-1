<p><strong>The Denim Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Denim</li><li>Medium Denim</li><li>Heavy Denim</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The denim market can be categorized into three primary types based on weight and fabric characteristics. Light denim, typically ranging from 7 to 10 ounces per square yard, is soft, breathable, and ideal for casual wear. Medium denim, weighing between 10 to 12 ounces, offers durability and versatility, suited for both casual and semi-formal attire. Heavy denim, exceeding 12 ounces, is robust and hard-wearing, often used for workwear and specialized apparel, providing maximum longevity and strength. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences and uses.</p></p>
